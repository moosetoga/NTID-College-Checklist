About this Checklist

This checklist lays out the steps you need to take during the college search and application process. Ask any college freshman—having all this information in one place while you are applying is a huge help. As you accomplish each step, check it off and fill in names, dates, times and comments for easy reference.

Remember, all colleges are different, and this information is general. Your college choice may require some additional steps, so investigate and add those to your checklist.
